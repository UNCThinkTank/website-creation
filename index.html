How we turn questions into decisions

Poli Plot Labs brings students, analysts, and designers together to pair economic modeling with narrative design. Every project moves through three connected tracks that keep your policy story rigorous, clear, and people-centered.

Insight Architecture

We frame the policy moment, define success, and build data frameworks that get to the real question.

  • Reviewing existing research, data quality, and blind spots
  • Identifying who matters, what they need, and how they’ll use the findings
  • Drafting fact sheets and key takeaways that tie evidence to decision

Plot Engineering

Analysts and designers iterate side by side to turn datasets into visual narratives shaped for your audience.

  • Explore scenarios and run checks that reveal what changes matter most
  • Build draft charts, storyboards, and interactives with quick feedback
  • Pair every figure with plain-language notes so evidence is easy to follow

Story Delivery

We make sure your policy story reaches the right audience in the right format.

  • Briefing decks and speaker notes for committees, classrooms, or press briefings
  • Web-ready articles and visuals that can drop straight into a site or newsletter
  • Sharing guides to help track engagement and amplify reach on social platforms
