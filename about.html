About Poli Plot Labs

Turning policy data into decisions people feel.

Policy research shouldn’t live in PDFs alone. We co-design visual stories with students, practitioners, and communities so insights travel faster and land with the people who can act.

The lab blends rapid data analysis with editorial design and context building. Every story we publish is grounded in method confidence, human language, and pathways for action.

Lab Snapshot

Where policy, design, and data storytellers meet.

We mentor student analysts through six-week studio cycles that pair them with policy partners. Each cohort produces visuals, narrative arcs, and activation kits ready to ship.

  • Insight studios Intake labs, framing workshops, and shared briefs to keep everyone aligned.
  • Evidence engine Reproducible notebooks, QA scripts, and reviewer checklists baked into every project.
  • Activation kits Storyboards, annotated visuals, and copy blocks tailored for newsletters, decks, and social.
  • 2 student storytellers in our pilot studio cohort.
  • 1 partner testing our decision-ready prototypes.
  • 3 policy domains in active build: energy, public health, and education.

For learners

Studio mentorship that sharpens data analysis, storytelling craft, and portfolio-ready deliverables.

For partners

Co-created insights that translate research into accessible language for stakeholders and decision makers.

For readers

Interactive explainers that deliver context, visuals, and actionable next steps in under five minutes.

Meet the Team

Led by policy storytellers who believe data belongs to community.

Poli Plot Labs is grounded in partnership. Co-founders Tristen and Bhairavi bring policy analysis, participatory design, and data storytelling expertise—and our studio alumni expand what’s possible with each build.

Tristen Best, MPP

Co-Founder · Energy Policy

Participatory energy resilience analyst who translates complex research into visual stories communities can use.

MPP graduate from UNC Chapel Hill with a background in economics and public policy. Tristen leads our participatory intake labs and ensures that every visual we publish is grounded in lived experience, plain language, and actionable framing for readers and decision makers alike.
