About Poli Plot Labs

We turn complex policy data into clear, visual stories— so leaders and communities can move from data to decision.

Research shows that most policy reports are rarely read beyond their executive summaries. Dense methods and technical language mean important findings often fail to reach the people who could use them. At the same time, students producing outstanding analysis in classrooms rarely get the chance to publish it for a wider audience.

Poli Plot Labs exists to bridge that disconnect.

For students, it’s a platform to practice data storytelling and showcase work to employers. For readers, it’s a way to understand pressing issues — from food security to energy resilience — in a visual, approachable way.

About

We apply economic analysis and data visualization to urgent topics in energy, food security, trade, and development. Each plot is concise, chart-forward, and built for impact.

In a noisy world, clarity is power. We cut through clutter with evidence-based storytelling—so decisions are faster, fairer, and easier to explain.
