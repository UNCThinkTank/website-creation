About Poli Plot Labs

Poli Plot Labs Plotting Policy for People

Policy research too often sits buried in PDFs. At Poli Plot Labs, we turn analysis into visual stories co-created with students, practitioners, and communities so insights travel faster and reach the people who matter.

Our approach blends rapid data analysis with editorial design and context building. Every piece we publish is rooted in methodological rigor, plain language, and clear pathways to action.

From projects to publishable stories.

Poli Plot Labs gives students a clear path to transform coursework and data into concise, visual policy stories. With practical frameworks, hands-on projects, and public showcases, every participant leaves with skills, confidence, and portfolio-ready work.

  • Project Frameworks. Templates and examples that show how to move from raw data to a clear narrative arc.
  • Project Sprints. Short, focused, original projects where students draft, refine, and publish policy stories utilizing data sources.
  • Project Spotlights. Students reframe class projects into concise, visual policy stories with takeaways for real-world audiences.
  • 2 student storytellers launching our pilot studio cohort.
  • 1 partner putting our decision ready prototypes into practice.
  • 3 policy domains in motion: energy, public health, and education.

Story Pipeline

From signal to story, a clear path for every idea.

At Poli Plot Labs, ideas do not sit still. Our communication funnel shows how a spark, whether a class project, dataset, or policy question, moves through intake, shaping, and publishing. At each stage we outline the goal, the collaborators involved, and the tools that keep momentum steady until the story is ready to share.

Meet the Team

Led by policy storytellers who believe data belongs to community.

We believe data belongs in the hands of communities. Poli Plot Labs is built on collaboration, where students, alumni, and cofounders work together to transform research into stories that travel. Each project adds new skills, new voices, and new ways of showing what policy can look like when it is shared clearly.

Our Journey

A growing lab with momentum.

Poli Plot Labs began with a simple idea: policy stories should move as quickly as the questions they answer. What started as an experiment in student storytelling is now a growing studio where research becomes visual, rigorous, and community centered. Here’s how the momentum is building.

May 2025 · Poli Plot Labs Ideation

Tristen and Bhairavi prototyped the first Poli Plot Labs concept after conclusion of graduate studies.

September 2025 · Pilot Studio Inaugural Post

The first official Poli Plot Labs post is made available for public viewing.

October 2025 · Partner Momentum [Current Phase]

Initial recruitment for university partners commences and growing number of contributors.

Looking Ahead · Amplifying Impact

We plan to expand the studio model, continuing to engage potential partners, and building an open network of students ready to showcase their skills.
